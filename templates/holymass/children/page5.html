D:

Christ Our Lord, save us all, through Your grace, make Your peace and tranquillity abound in us and have mercy on us.

All:

Lord, have mercy on us.

D:

Let us commend ourselves and one another to the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit.

All:

Lord our God we commend ourselves to you.

C:

Lord, Almighty God, we humbly implore You. Fill us with Your merciful grace. Pour forth Your gifts through our hands. May Your blessings and grace obtain forgiveness of debts, and remission of sins for the flock You have chosen in Your infinite mercy. The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, Lord of all, for ever.

All:

Amen.

D:

Bless us O Lord; Brothers and sisters, bow your heads for the imposition of hands and receive the blessing.

C:

Lord, our God! Extend Your merciful right hand over the universal and apostolic Church. Protect it from every danger, visible and invisible. In Your compassion make us worthy to minister in Your presence with devotion, diligence and purity.

C:

Merciful God! Bless us. Grant that all of us, as one body, may properly please You throughout our lives, by works of justice, that reconciles us with You. Make us worthy to offer You never ending praise, homage, thanksgiving and adoration. The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, Lord of all, for ever.

All:

Amen.

D:

Let all, who have been baptized and sealed with the sign of life, participate in these holy mysteries attentively and devoutedly.

Onitha d’Raze (Anthem of the Mysteries)

All:

"In the Lord I put my firm trust"

Here is our Lord’s precious Body and Blood
With love and respect
Let us gather by this altar
Let us all join with the heavenly choir
And sing, God is Holy
Holy, Holy forever.

Or

All:

I have firmly trusted in the Lord. The precious Body and Blood of Christ/ is on the holy Altar. Let us all approach him/ with reverence and awe/ and sing His praises with the angels: Holy, Holy, Holy Lord.

Onitha d’raze II [Prose]

C:

Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit. Let there be the commemoration of the Blessed Virgin Mary, Mother of God, and St. Joseph the just, at this holy altar.

All:

From the eternity and forever, Amen. Apostles and friends/ of the only begotten Son of God, pray for peace in the world.

C:

Let the people of God proclaim Amen, Amen. Let us celebrate at this altar the memory of our Father in faith St Thomas the Apostle, together with the just who have triumphed and the martyrs who have been crowned in glory.

All:

The mighty Lord is with us; our King is with us; our God is with us. The Lord of Jacob is our Help.
